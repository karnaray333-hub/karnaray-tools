What Does Merging PDF Files Mean?

When you merge PDF files, you combine two or more separate PDF documents into one single PDF file. For example, if you have a resume as one PDF and a cover letter as another PDF, merging them creates one combined document that you can send as a single attachment. This makes it much easier to share, print and manage your documents.

PDF merging is used in many everyday situations. Students combine assignment pages and reference materials. Business professionals merge invoices, contracts and reports. Job seekers combine their resume, certificates and portfolio. Lawyers and accountants merge case documents and financial records. With a free online PDF merger, all of these tasks can be completed in just a few clicks.
