What is Image Compressor?

Image Compressor is a free online tool that reduces the file size of your images without significant loss in visual quality. Large image files can slow down websites, take up storage space and be difficult to share. Our tool lets you compress images quickly, helping you save storage space and improve website loading speed. Why compress your images?

  • Smaller images load faster on websites and apps

  • Saves storage space on your device and cloud storage
  • Better performance for web developers and bloggers

Password Generator - Create Strong Secure Passwords Free
Generate random strong passwords for your accounts

What is Password Generator?

The Password Generator is a free online tool that creates strong, random and secure passwords for your online accounts. Using weak or reused passwords is one of the biggest security risks online. Our tool generates passwords with a combination of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers and special symbols, making them extremely difficult to guess or crack.

What makes a password strong?
